What I am all about.

I am a PhD student at IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay, India.
The research work is being supervised by Dr. Jayesh S. Pillai.

I am skilled in User Experience Design, Augmented/Virtual Reality, HCI and Educational Technology. My reasearch interest lies in establishing Augmented Reality technology in school education. I am interested in investigating the design strategies for Augmented Reality based learning experience in K-12 classrooms.
